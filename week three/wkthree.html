Bourns Family Youth Innovation Center

About

The Youth Innovation Center is the City of Riverside's newest community center! With state of the art equipment and a mission to help propel Riverside into an innovative tomorrow, the center will offer both free and fee based activities. Our mission is to provide opportunities to visitors by introducing them to professionals and services that will promote and enhance learning to increase the likelihood of life-long success. Our focus is to prepare all Riverside residents for success by providing a safe environment that fosters collaboration, learning and creativity.

Classes

Web Dev Class

The Bourns Family Youth Innovation Center is offering a wide variety of virtual classes. Students get a chance to tour the new Bourns Family Youth Innovation Center while learning to develop a website with multiple pages. Learn how to document and save code just like software engineers in the field. Software and hardware needed are all free.

Virtual Video Game Building

The Bourns Family Youth Innovation Center is offering a wide variety of virtual programs. Blocky style coding is a way to learn major programming easily. Learn to create your own arcade style game with Microsoft Make Code which teaches basic concepts of both game design and programming structure. All you'll need is a computer with internet access and camera to participate in the virtual classroom.
